FIRETEAM BRAVO: ONLINE
Realistic Tactical FPS. Squad-Based Combat. Built for Competition.

The Hook

FIRETEAM BRAVO Online is a realistic tactical FPS built in Unreal Engine 5 that emphasizes authentic military combat and squad-based coordination. Players engage in objective-driven multiplayer matches with realistic weapon handling, advanced movement mechanics, and strategic gameplay that rewards teamwork over run-and-gun tactics.
Key features include dedicated server support with Epic Online Services integration, crossplay functionality, realistic ballistics and damage systems, and a focus on immersive mil-sim experience without sacrificing accessibility for competitive FPS players.

Gameplay Overview

Tactical Combat

Strategic, objective-based multiplayer matches where every decision matters. Players must coordinate with their squad, clear rooms methodically, and approach each engagement with tactical precision.

Realistic Gunplay

Authentic weapon handling with realistic ballistics, recoil patterns, and damage models. Each weapon feels unique and requires mastery to use effectively in combat scenarios.

Squad Coordination

Built from the ground up for team play. Communication, positioning, and coordinated tactics are essential for success. Lone wolves don't survive.

Advanced Movement

Fluid movement system including tactical sprint, leaning, mounting, and breach mechanics. Movement is deliberate and contextual, not arcade-style.

Competitive Ready

Balanced gameplay designed for competitive esports potential. Ranked matchmaking, spectator mode, and skill-based progression systems.

Crossplay Enabled

Epic Online Services integration enables seamless crossplay between PC, PlayStation, and Xbox. One unified playerbase across all platforms.
